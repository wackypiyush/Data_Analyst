Indian Crime Analysis

The India Crime dataset contains information about various types of crimes reported in different states and districts of India during 2001 to 2012. It allows for analyzing crime trends, identifying hotspots, and understanding the severity and magnitude of crimes.
This dataset analysis is been deployed or shown using streamlit framework.

Hyundai Car Features

Scraped data from Kaggle on UK Hyundai car listings from 2000-2020. Economic impact on listing and evolution of a customer based on the launched features and demands can help company make better decisions on what to offer. The conclusions can also help a seller to decide the price of a car while listing.

MedHive

MedHive, a Hospital Assist platform is a web-based application that provides a convenient way for patients to access medical services, such as booking beds and scheduling appointments, especially during times of high demand. The platform uses a combination of modern technologies, including React, Next.JS, Tailwind CSS, and Python with Uvicorn, Fast API, and FastAPI middleware.cors. The database is used is MSSQL.
